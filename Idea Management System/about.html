Frequently Asked Questions

How do I submit a new idea?

Navigate to the "My Ideas" page and click the "New Idea" button. Fill in all the required fields including title, description, category, and ratings, then submit.

What do the different stages mean?

Idea: Initial concept stage
Screening: Under evaluation
Prototype: In development
Implementation: Being deployed

How does the scoring system work?

Ideas are rated on three metrics (1-5): Impact (potential effect), Feasibility (ease of implementation), and Overall Rating. The screening score is the average of impact and feasibility.

Can I edit or delete my ideas?

Yes! Go to "My Ideas" page where you can edit or delete any of your submitted ideas using the action buttons on each idea card.

What are the different categories?

Product: New products or features
Process: Workflow and efficiency improvements
Marketing: Campaigns and promotional ideas
Social: Community and social impact initiatives

How can I see analytics?

Visit the "Analytics" page to view comprehensive reports including category distribution, top ideas, contributor rankings, and activity timeline.
